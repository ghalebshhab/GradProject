Project Overview

What is JO MAP?

JO MAP is a smart location and activity-based community platform designed for users across Jordan. It enables users to discover places, join activities, and interact within a digital community through an interactive map-based experience.

Project Motivation

People in Jordan face difficulties finding reliable and organized information about locations and activities. Existing platforms are fragmented, focusing only on maps or events without supporting meaningful community interaction.

JO MAP was created to provide one unified platform that combines location discovery, activity participation, and social interaction.

Problem Statement

There is no unified digital platform in Jordan that integrates locations, activities, and community interaction while providing accurate and up-to-date information and allowing businesses to manage their digital presence effectively.

This results in low user engagement, limited awareness of activities, and a scattered user experience.

Project Aim

The aim of JO MAP is to design a comprehensive digital platform that enables users to discover locations, participate in activities, and engage socially within a single environment tailored to the Jordanian context.

Project Objectives

Requirements Analysis

Analyze the requirements of users, business owners, and administrators to ensure an effective and inclusive system design.

Interactive Map System

Design an interactive map-based platform to support location discovery and activity participation.

Community Engagement

Support ratings, comments, and community posts to enhance user interaction and trust.

Business Management

Enable business owners to manage their locations, activities, and digital presence efficiently.

Moderation & Reporting

Implement moderation and reporting mechanisms to maintain content quality and platform safety.

Scalability & Localization

Support Arabic and English interfaces and propose a scalable system architecture for future growth.

Problem Statement

Why JoMap is Needed

Unverified Locations

Many platforms allow users to post locations without validation, resulting in fake or misleading information.

Poor Local Discovery

Users struggle to find nearby events or activities happening right now.

Lack of Moderation

Existing systems lack proper reporting and administrative control.

No Direct Chat

Users cannot communicate directly with others, limiting engagement and community building.

Missed Events

Users often miss important or nearby events due to poor notifications and event visibility.

Fragmented Experience

Users have to switch between multiple apps for maps, social interaction, and event updates.

JoMap Solution

How We Solve the Problems

Verified Locations

Locations and events are reviewed and moderated before becoming public, ensuring users access only trustworthy information.

Admin Moderation

Admin dashboard allows monitoring users, posts, events, and reports, keeping the community safe and reliable.

Reporting System

Users can report fake or inappropriate content directly from the app, helping maintain content quality.

Direct Chat

Users can communicate with each other privately or in groups, enabling better community interaction.

Event Notifications

Receive timely alerts about nearby and upcoming events based on interests and location.

All-in-One Experience

JoMap combines maps, community posts, chats, and events into a seamless platform—no need to switch between apps.
